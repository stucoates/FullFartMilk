Welcome to Full Fart Milk

This site provides information about which farms, suppliers, producers, and supermarkets are selling products from animals that may have been fed feed additives, such as Bovaer® which is being trialed by Arla Foods.

No claims are made about possible harms or otherwise that these feed additives may cause, but we absolutely believe this information should be available to consumers who can make their own informed purchasing decisions.

This site is not run or sponsored by any industry body or organisation, it has been set up purely to provide information, not marketing messages or industry propoganda. If you'd like to support this site, please consider buying yourself a mug.

The information on this site has mostly been compiled from social media and reader submitted reports. If you would like to correct or add information, please email info@fullfartmilk.info.
