Welcome to Full Fart Milk

This site has been set up to provide information about which farms, suppliers, producers, and supermarkets are selling products from animals that may have been fed feed additives, such as Bovaer® which is being trialed by Arla Foods.

We make no claims as to possible harms or otherwise that these feed additives may cause, but absolutely believe this information should be available so people can make their own informed purchasing decisions.

This site is not run or sponsored by any industry body or organisation, it has been set up purely to provide information, not marketing messages or industry propoganda. If you'd like to support this site, please consider buying yourself a mug.

The information on this site has mostly been harvested from social media. If you would like to correct or add information, please email info@fullfartmilk.info.
